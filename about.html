ISSVP INFO

ISSVP is a project under IEEE SIESGST aims to provide comprehensive support and solution to the impoverished communities of the nation. The main goal of this smart village project is to pave a path with a broad scope of opportunities and resources that will be integrated with energy, education, and sustainable development in order to empower these off-grid communities.

ISSVP comprises of a team from various domains namely, telecommunication, data management, solar power management, teaching and to impart knowledge. As a kickstart, the team takes a step forward in transforming the Virjoli Village located in the Raigarh district of Maharashtra in India.

“It is in your hands to make a better world for all those who live in it”

- Nelson Mandela

The Smart village executers believe in gaining the respect and confidence of the localists of the village. In doing so, their motive is to abide by preserving the traditions and cultures of the area while they subtly introduce the various technologies and its advancements. Like so, this development would ameliorate their existing resources without hampering their style and standards of living.

This project will help the village to overcome its hurdles by bringing it closer to its success path through electricity and the cellular network. In the process of enhancing its assets, it will be introduced to the new digital trends as well as sustainable agricultural technologies through a variety of STEM lectures. This will help the village and its people to be self-reliant, making a step towards becoming ‘ATMANIRBHAR BHARAT’.
