ISSVP INFO

ISSVP is a project under IEEE SIESGST aims to provide comprehensive support and solution to the impoverished communities of the nation. The main goal of this smart village project is to pave a path with a broad scope of opportunities and resources that will be integrated with energy, education, and sustainable development in order to empower these off-grid communities.

ISSVP comprises of a team from various domains namely, telecommunication, data management, solar power management, teaching and to impart knowledge. As a kickstart, the team takes a step forward in transforming the Virjoli Village located in the Raigarh district of Maharashtra in India.

“It is in your hands to make a better world for all those who live in it”

- Nelson Mandela

The Smart village executers believe in gaining the respect and confidence of the localists of the village. In doing so, their motive is to abide by preserving the traditions and cultures of the area while they subtly introduce the various technologies and its advancements. Like so, this development would ameliorate their existing resources without hampering their style and standards of living.

This project will help the village to overcome its hurdles by bringing it closer to its success path through electricity and the cellular network. In the process of enhancing its assets, it will be introduced to the new digital trends as well as sustainable agricultural technologies through a variety of STEM lectures. This will help the village and its people to be self-reliant, making a step towards becoming ‘ATMANIRBHAR BHARAT’.

IEEE SIES GST

IEEE SIESGST is one of the oldest, technical student branches of SIES GST that was established in the year 2007 under the IEEE Bombay Section. We imbibe to provide an opportunity to explore, learn, innovate and implement new technological advancements and ideologies. It an active body in organizing all co-curricular and extra-curricular activities in the college.
IEEE SIESGST focusses on providing a path to each of its members and students, not only to enhance their erudite in technology and academics but also helps them to emerge as individual leaders. In doing so, by every passing year, IEEE SIESGST has been witnessing a growth by leaps and bounds.

“It is a great platform to build your technical skills “We don’t see things as they are, we see them as we are”

--- IEEE SIESGST ALUMNI

IEEE SIESGST, proudly launched its sub chapter Microwave Theory and Techniques Society (MTT-S) as well as an affinity group, Women in Engineering (WiE) in recent times. In a nutshell it serves as a one stage for all to attend workshops, seminars, Industrial Visits conducted by many well qualified and trained expertise. Leadership and perseverance are reflected in each of its team members who put in a collective effort to work towards the betterment of this student branch. Needless to say, the success of national level annual technical festival called ‘Techopedia’ puts the student branch on the pinnacle. IEEE SIESGST attributes all of its achievements and success to its branch counsellor, Mr. Biju Balakrishnan who is the backbone and pillar of strength to the core team and all its 121 members.
